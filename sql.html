YELLEVATE CUSTOMER DISPUTE ANALYSIS

Data cleaning and verification for Yellevate Customer Disputes Analysis.

Goal

a. The processing time in which Invoices are settled (average # of days rounded to a whole number).

b. The processing time for the company to settle disputes (average # of days rounded to a whole number).

c. Percentage of disputes received by the company that w ere lost (within two decimal places).

d. Percentage of revenue lost from disputes (within two decimal places).

e. The country where the company reached the highest losses from lost disputes (in USD).

SQL Query

a. SELECT ROUND(AVG(days_to_settled)) AS invoice_avg_days_settled FROM yellevate_invoices;

b.SELECT ROUND(AVG(days_to_settled)) AS dispute_ave_days_settled FROM yellevate_invoices WHERE disputed = 1;

c. SELECT (ROUND(100*((SELECT SUM(dispute_lost) FROM yellevate_invoices WHERE dispute_lost = 1) / (SELECT SUM(disputed) FROM yellevate_invoices) ),2)) || '%' AS percent_dispute_lost;

d.SELECT (ROUND(100*( (SELECT SUM(invoice_amount_usd) FROM yellevate_invoices WHERE dispute_lost = 1) / (SELECT SUM(invoice_amount_usd) FROM yellevate_invoices)),2)) || '%' AS percent_revenue_lost;

e.SELECT country, SUM(invoice_amount_usd):: MONEY AS total_losses FROM yellevate_invoices WHERE dispute_lost = 1 GROUP BY country ORDER BY total_losses DESC;
